Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

1. Comprehensive Data Collection

At ZenTail, we recognize that your privacy and the privacy of your pets are of paramount importance. We collect data to provide a seamless veterinary connection service. This data collection is categorized as follows:

1.1 User Personal Information

We collect identifiable information including your full name, primary email address, and verified mobile number. This data is essential for maintaining your user profile and allowing clinics to contact you regarding your pet's health status.

1.2 Pet Biological and Health Data

To assist veterinarians in preparing for your visit, we store profiles for your animals, including name, species, breed, age, weight, and existing medical conditions or allergies. This information is encrypted and only transmitted to a clinic when a booking request is initiated.

2. Geolocation and Map Services

Our "Vet Locator" tool utilizes the Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap APIs. When you select "Use My Location," your browser coordinates are processed to find the nearest partner clinics. We do not maintain a permanent log of your precise movements; location data is session-based and discarded upon closing the application.

Data Security Guarantee: ZenTail employs AES-256 encryption for all database entries stored in MongoDB Atlas. We perform weekly security audits to ensure your pet's medical history remains confidential.

3. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to marketing firms. Data is only shared with licensed veterinary clinics that you choose to interact with. We may disclose data if required by law to prevent animal cruelty or to comply with a judicial proceeding.

Appointment & Liability Policy

1. The Booking Process

ZenTail facilitates the communication between the pet owner and the clinic. A booking submitted via our "Book a Slot" form is considered a "Request" and does not guarantee a reserved time until the status on your dashboard changes to "Approved".

1.1 Status Definitions

  • Pending: Your request is waiting for clinic review.
  • Confirmed/Approved: The clinic has officially blocked the time for you.
  • Cancelled: The clinic is unable to fulfill the request. A reason (e.g., "Doctor Unavailable") will be provided in the tracking dashboard.
